General Meetings are held six times per year. All are open to the community — members of the general public are welcome in a listen-only capacity.

For administrators: The meetings list is powered by a Google Sheet. Open assets/js/main.js, find MEETINGS_SHEET_URL, and replace the placeholder with your published CSV link. Columns needed: date | title | description | slides_link | recording_link | type. Sample entries are shown until then.
Open meetings: All General Meetings are open to the entire ISRU UK community. Members of the general public are welcome to attend in a listen-only capacity. Upcoming meetings are announced via the JISCMail list.
