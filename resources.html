Resources

Useful links, documents, and information for the UK ISRU community.

🏛 UK Space & Government

  • 🔗
    UK Space Agency The government agency responsible for the UK's civil space programme.
  • 📄
    National Space Strategy (2021) Sets out the UK government's long-term ambitions for the space sector, including ISRU as a priority area.
  • 📄
    Space Exploration Technology Roadmap (2023) UKSA roadmap identifying ISRU as a key technology area for UK investment and development.

🌍 International Agencies

  • 🔗
    European Space Agency (ESA) Europe's gateway to space — a key partner for UK ISRU research and mission opportunities.
  • 🔗
    NASA ISRU Programme NASA's In-Situ Resource Utilisation programme, developing technologies for Moon and Mars exploration.
  • 🔗
    UNCOPUOS UN Committee on the Peaceful Uses of Outer Space — the international forum for space law and policy.

🤝 Community & Networks

  • ✉
    Planetary ISRU JISCMail The primary mailing list for the UK ISRU community. Free to join — this constitutes formal ISRU UK membership.
  • 🔗
    Lunar Surface Innovation Consortium (LSIC) A US-based consortium working on lunar surface technology, including ISRU. A useful model for community organisation.
  • 🔗
    Space Generation Advisory Council (SGAC) International network representing students and young professionals in the space sector.

📅 Key Conferences

  • 🔗
    Space Resources Week (SRW) Annual international conference dedicated to space resources, held in Luxembourg. A key forum for ISRU UK members.
  • 🔗
    Lunar and Planetary Science Conference (LPSC) Annual conference covering all aspects of planetary science, including lunar resources and ISRU technologies.
  • 🔗
    International Astronautical Congress (IAC) The world's largest space conference — a major venue for presenting UK ISRU work internationally.
