People

The Management Committee and Directorate Heads who coordinate the ISRU UK network.

Leadership

Management Committee

The Management Committee oversees the strategic direction and day-to-day governance of ISRU UK. Positions rotate through a structured progression to ensure continuity and fresh perspectives.

ℹ  The Management Committee is currently transitioning to the structure described in the Terms of Reference. The below reflects the current and incoming committee.
👤
Chair Emeritus
Dr Hannah Sargeant
University of Leicester

Hannah is a researcher at the University of Leicester. She has a PhD in Planetary Science investigating water production from lunar regolith for the ESA PROSPECT payload. Her research includes icy simulant development and novel applications of space nuclear power systems.

👤
Chair
Prof. Patrick Harkness
University of Glasgow

Patrick is Professor of Exploration Technology at the University of Glasgow. His research focuses on the behaviour and fluidisation of granular materials, with applications in polar-terrestrial and planetary surface environments, and space propulsion.

👤
Co-Chair A
Dr Joshua Rasera
Imperial College London

Joshua is a postdoctoral researcher in the Space and Terrestrial Resource Group at Imperial College London. His research focuses on developing sustainable mineral beneficiation techniques for terrestrial mining and advancing mineral processing methods for lunar and extraterrestrial environments.

Prof. Ian Crawford
Birkbeck, University of London

Professor of Planetary Science and Astrobiology. His research focuses on lunar exploration, remote sensing of the lunar surface, analysis of lunar samples, and the prospects for lunar resources.

Lead of the Space Systems research group. Over 15 years' experience in space technologies, with PI roles on ESA, UKSA, and EPSRC projects focused on lunar ISRU excavation technologies.
